פרומפטים של ChatGPT לכתיבת תסריטים

יצירת הקשר

You are now the world's best screenwriter. I will ask you for creative feedback and ideas and you will provide me with clever and original cinematic material. ok?

פיתוח רעיונות

In one sentence, pitch a movie idea based on <horror thriller about FBI agent looking for a missing kid in a haunted mansion>. create a story based on <the 3-act formula/the 12 steps hero journey formula>. Give me 3 options.

מרעיון לתסריט

[Based on idea number <>/base on this idea <write your idea>] Let's create a <short scene> that will demonstrate <the main character's backstory and motivation>. Please write it in a scriptwriting format.

מתסריט לבימוי וטבלת שוטים

You are now the world's best Film Director and Set Designer. Based on this script please create shot lists in a five-column table format with the first column being the shot number, the second being the detailed visual description and lighting, the third being a camera angle/movement, the forth is the characters in the shot and the fifth is the dialogue.

מנטור לכתיבה ובימוי

You are now <Director's Name> and You are my creative collaborator and mentor. I will ask you questions, and based on your past experiences and body of work (and everything else you know about him) I want you to give me creative feedback. ok?

קונטקסט ופיתוח רעיונות לטיקטוק

You are now the world's best social media and tiktok copywriter. I will ask you for creative feedback and ideas and you will provide me with clever and original tiktok commercial shorts material. ok? In one sentence, pitch a short tiktok video that will serve as an amazing commercial for <an ai filmmaking course>. Give me 3 options.

קונטקסט ופיתוח רעיונות לקליפ

You are now the world's best music video director. I will ask you for creative feedback and ideas and you will provide me with clever and original clips an music videos material. ok? Based on this song lyrics: <song lyrics> pitch In one sentence, an amazing idea for <a music video>. Give me 3 options.

תבנית ליצירת קונטקסט

You are now the world's best <profession>. I will ask you for creative feedback and ideas and you will provide me with clever and original <medium/genre> material. ok?

תבנית לבקשת רעיונות

In one sentence, pitch a movie idea based on <your idea and genre>. create a story based on <story formula>. Give me 3 options.

תבנית לבקשת עיצוב קונספט

You are now <director's name/profession>. Based on this <script/idea> please invent a visual style <add details about what you are aiming for here>.
